Our Story

Built by believers in honest love

The Open Heart started as a conversation between two friends who were tired of pretending relationships were simple. We wanted a place where people could be honest about love without being judged — where your pain could find words, and your words could find a home.

Today we're a growing community of writers, seekers, and lovers — people who believe that being vulnerable takes courage, and courage deserves to be celebrated.

We write back. We publish. We sell things that actually help. And we do all of it with care.
